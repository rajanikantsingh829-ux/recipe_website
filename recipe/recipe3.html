Malai Chaap

Preparation Time: 35 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Soya chaap sticks
  • Fresh cream
  • Curd
  • Butter
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cut chaap into pieces.
  2. Marinate with curd, cream, spices.
  3. Cook in butter.
  4. Stir until creamy texture forms.
  5. Serve hot.
