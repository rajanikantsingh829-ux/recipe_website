Veg Biryani

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Prepare vegetable gravy.
  3. Layer rice and gravy.
  4. Cook on low flame.
  5. Serve hot.
