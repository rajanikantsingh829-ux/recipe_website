Maggie

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggie noodles
  • 1½ cups water
  • Maggie tastemaker
  • Chopped vegetables (optional)
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add noodles and tastemaker.
  3. Add vegetables if desired.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes while stirring.
  5. Serve hot.
