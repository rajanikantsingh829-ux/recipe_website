Rasgulla

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil milk and curdle it.
  2. Strain and make soft dough.
  3. Make small balls.
  4. Boil in sugar syrup.
  5. Serve chilled.
