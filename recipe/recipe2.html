White Sauce Pasta

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt, pepper
  • Cheese
  • Mixed vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil pasta and keep aside.
  2. Melt butter, add flour and cook.
  3. Add milk slowly and stir.
  4. Add salt, pepper, cheese.
  5. Add pasta and mix well.
  6. Serve hot.
